ABOUT AAROHAN

Aarohan is the annual sports fest of Indian Institute of Technology, Ropar, one among the most reputed institutes in the country. Since its inception in 2016, Aarohan has unceasingly progressed each year. Spread across three days, Aarohan, IIT Ropar’s flagship sporting fest, is nothing short of a sporty pageant.

Aarohan attracts a wide spectrum of the youth from all over north India by providing excellent organizational infrastructure facilities, grueling competition and motivation to enable students achieve the zenith of their respective sports performances. Having witnessed over 600 participants in the third edition, the fest has provided students from different colleges and institutes an opportunity to participate and explore.

The fest incorporates a wide range of outdoor as well as indoor sports activities like cricket, badminton, volleyball, basketball, football, tennis, chess, table tennis, and athletics events. Every year it is our ardent desire to make the fest bigger and better and with the same vision, the 3 days Fest is scheduled to kick start from 15 March 2019. In its fourth edition, Aarohan’19 promises to take a new avatar and bring up the competition to a whole new level of competition which encompasses a variety of tournaments with an aim to create the ultimate synergy in the latest edition of this exhilarating fest.

We invite you to be a part of this sensational journey that will not only grip your nerves but also electrify your soul. Come, feel the adrenaline rush as you battle it out to be crowned the best of the best.
