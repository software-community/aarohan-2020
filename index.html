ABOUT AAROHAN

Indian Institute of Technology, Ropar is proud to introduce the fifth edition of its annual flagship event of sports, the inter collegiate sports fest AAROHAN being organized from 20th March to 22nd March, 2020.

With the official tagline “Sports for all” the fifth edition of AAROHAN will witness plethora of neck to neck competition in sporting events mentioned in the brochure attached along with. We look forward to bringing the best athletes and teams from various institutions under the same umbrella to exhibit their talent and not just win laurels to their institution but also contribute in improving the culture of sports in other nearby institutions through the means of our fest.

What’s new this year ?
  • The Fest is being organized for the first time in the excellent well-furnished sporting facilities of the sprawling new campus of IIT Ropar.
  • Our national game Hockey too has been introduced this year and we look forward to organize it successfully with even more enthusiasm than previous years.
  • Points for each event will be added up for all institutes and the contingent with maximum points will be awarded the general championship trophy.

Since AAROHAN is not just a tournament but a fest there is more than enthralling core sports events to it. Just like the previous editions, other informal events like DJ nights, bonfires, gala dinner and many other fun events will be organized for the athletes to relax and have fun after a long strenuous day of matches.

Keeping up with our tag line “Sports for all”, AAROHAN’20 team wants to give back to the society and create an impact by promoting sports as a means of building confidence, helping unify, empower and motivate various deprived communities through the means of awareness campaigns, runs, cycle rallies, Honorary matches for children with special needs and other such events.
